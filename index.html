FAINT

A first person Horror-Walking Simulator made with Unreal Engine 5 set in a seemingly abandoned Hotel in the 1960's in Eastern Germany. Explore the building, find clues and unravel a unforseen mystery.

As the Game's Tech-Lead it was my obligation to ensure it runs smoothly, implement more difficult mechanics like Portals, as well as implement Level-Streaming.

The Purrfect Brew

The Purrfect Brew is a top-down Bubble-Tea-Shop Management Game where you prepare and serve bubble-tea to impatient customers. It was made in Unreal Engine 5.4.4 using Blueprints.

My job as the tech-lead was distributing tasks among the other programmers, helping them with problems they were having as well as programming a lot myself.

My main focus points were the movement of the player-characters, the AI that contolled the Customers' movement and the interaction with objects.

Fearless Ava

Fearless Ava is a 2D Plattformer Game, where a  mother fox has to manage her job as an Adventurer as well as her family life. The Game was made using the Unity Engine.

My tasks as Game-Mechanic Programmer involved all enemies and the  system controlling them, as well as some object interactions. Additionally I created the UI, and a System to save Game-Data which, due to time cuts, did not make it into the released version.

New Horizon

An interactive Simulation made in collaboration with the "Ars Electronica Center" in Linz, Austria. Their Deepspace Room, equipped with LIDAR sensor technology, allowed us to develop this breathtaking visualisation, depicting how a message gets sent between mobile phones in a simplified matter.

We showcased it at the venue's annual festival, where hundreds of people could play it live or watch others play. Additionally, it won third place at a multi-regional competition.
